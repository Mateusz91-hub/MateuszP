About Me!

Introduction

Hello, everyone; my name is Mateusz Malerek. I am a Furniture Maker with a longstanding passion for technology. Despite an initial career path rooted in craftsmanship, software development has always fascinated me. A year ago, I began pursuing a computing degree at Arden University to fulfil my dream of becoming a professional AI engineer. I am eager to gain the knowledge and experience necessary to excel in artificial intelligence and web application development, leveraging my unique blend of creativity and technical skills.

Professional Experience

As a Furniture Maker at The Senator Group Furniture Workshop, I have designed and handcrafted custom furniture for residential and commercial clients, ensuring high-quality finishes and meticulous attention to detail. My role involves collaborating closely with clients to understand their needs and preferences and translating them into functional and aesthetically pleasing furniture. I utilize various woodworking tools and techniques to create durable and stylish furniture, managing multiple projects simultaneously to ensure timely delivery and client satisfaction. Previously, as an Apprentice Furniture Maker at Craftsman's Workshop, I assisted senior furniture makers in designing and producing custom furniture. This role allowed me to gain proficiency in using woodworking machinery and hand tools, learn traditional and modern furniture-making techniques, and participate in selecting and preparing materials to ensure optimal quality and sustainability.

Education and Skills

I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computing at Arden University. My coursework includes Web Development, Data Structures, Algorithms, and Database Systems. Before this, I completed a Diploma in Furniture Making, where I studied Woodworking Techniques, Furniture Design, Material Science, and Project Management. In addition, I've been taking programming courses on Udemy in languages such as JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Python, and SQL, as well as learning CAD software like AutoCAD and SketchUp. I have foundational knowledge of AI and machine learning concepts and am proficient in web development tools. My craftsmanship skills encompass woodworking, furniture design, joinery, finishing, and material selection. Additionally, I possess strong soft skills in creativity, problem-solving, project management, communication, and collaboration.

Current Projects

I am developing a web application to enhance my portfolio, implementing features using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to create an interactive user experience. This project integrates a backend database with SQL to store and manage user data. Additionally, I am utilizing Python and machine learning libraries to develop and train a model, conducting user testing to refine the recommendation algorithm and improve its accuracy.

Conclusion

In conclusion, my journey from Furniture Maker to aspiring AI engineer is driven by a passion for technology and continuous learning. My experience in craftsmanship and growing expertise in software development and AI positions me well for the tech industry. My background in designing custom furniture and developing web applications demonstrates my commitment to quality and innovation. As I continue my studies at Arden University and enhance my technical skills, I am eager to apply my creativity and analytical abilities to AI and web development, contributing to innovative projects and technological advancements.
